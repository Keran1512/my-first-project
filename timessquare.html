Times Square: the heart of New York City

Times Square is one of the most iconic destinations in New York City, known for its dazzling lights, massive digital billboards, and nonstop energy. Located at the intersection of Broadway and Seventh Avenue in Midtown Manhattan, it is a hub for entertainment, shopping, and dining.

Top Attractions in Times Square

Broadway Theatres

Home to world-famous Broadway shows, Times Square is a must-visit for theater lovers. Popular venues include the Richard Rodgers Theatre (Hamilton) and the Minskoff Theatre (The Lion King).

The Times Square Billboards

The massive LED screens make Times Square one of the brightest places on Earth at night. The "New Year's Eve Ball Drop" happens here annually at One Times Square.

Madame Tussauds New York

This wax museum features lifelike figures of celebrities, athletes, and historical figures, providing plenty of great photo opportunities.

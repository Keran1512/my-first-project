Eiffel Tower-A must-see landmark in Paris

No trip to Paris is complete without visiting the Eiffel Tower, one of the world’s most iconic landmarks! Standing 330 meters tall, this breathtaking iron masterpiece offers unparalleled panoramic views of the City of Light.



What to Expect

Three viewing levels

Enjoy stunning sights from the first and second floors, or take the elevator to the summit for a 360° view of Paris.

Sparkling Night Show

Watch the Eiffel Tower light up every evening with its dazzling light display.

Dinner with a View

Savor fine French cuisine at Madame Brasserie or indulge in a luxurious meal at the Michelin-starred Le Jules Verne restaurant.

Perfect for photos

Capture unforgettable moments at Trocadéro Gardens, Champ de Mars, or from the Seine River on a cruise.
